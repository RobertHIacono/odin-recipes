Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a simple pasta recipe

ingredients

  • Pasta - spaghetti, penne, or farfalle
  • Jarred sauce
  • Can of fish
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
    •
    1. Boil water
    2. Add pasta and cook
    3. Remove Pasta and drain
    4. Add olive oil, sauce, fish, salt, and pepper to empty pot and bring to a boil
    5. Add pasta to pot and stir
    6. Fin.
